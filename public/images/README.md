# Hero Background Image

## Required Image

Place your high-quality wedding/engagement photo here:

**Filename:** `hero-bg.jpg`  
**Location:** `wedding-site/public/images/hero-bg.jpg`

### Recommended Specifications:
- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 or higher (Full HD+)
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 or wider
- **File Size:** Under 2MB (compressed)
- **Quality:** High resolution, well-lit photo

### Suggested Images:
- Engagement venue exterior
- Beautiful Indian wedding decor
- Marble rocks or Jabalpur landmarks
- Traditional wedding elements (mandap, flowers)
- Couple photo (pre-wedding shoot)

### Tips:
- Use a photo with good contrast for text overlay
- Avoid photos that are too busy or cluttered
- Ensure the image works well with the maroon/gold overlay

---

**Once you add the image, the Hero section will display it with:**
- Parallax scrolling effect
- Luxurious maroon gradient overlay
- Gold typography that's perfectly readable
