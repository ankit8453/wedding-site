# Required Images for Wedding Website

## 1. Hero Background Image

**Filename:** `hero-bg.jpg`  
**Location:** `wedding-site/public/images/hero-bg.jpg`

### Recommended Specifications:
- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 or higher (Full HD+)
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 or wider
- **File Size:** Under 2MB (compressed)
- **Quality:** High resolution, well-lit photo

### Suggested Images:
- Engagement venue exterior
- Beautiful Indian wedding decor
- Marble rocks or Jabalpur landmarks
- Traditional wedding elements (mandap, flowers)
- Couple photo (pre-wedding shoot)

### Tips:
- Use a photo with good contrast for text overlay
- Avoid photos that are too busy or cluttered
- Ensure the image works well with the maroon/gold overlay

**Display:** Parallax scrolling with luxurious maroon gradient overlay

## 2. Official Invitation Card ✨ NEW

**Filename:** `official-invite.jpg`  
**Location:** `wedding-site/public/images/official-invite.jpg`

### Recommended Specifications:
- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Resolution:** 1200x1600 or higher (Portrait orientation)
- **Aspect Ratio:** 3:4 or similar portrait ratio
- **File Size:** Under 1.5MB (compressed)
- **Quality:** High resolution, crisp text

### Tips:
- Should be the actual invitation card design
- Portrait orientation works best
- Ensure all text is readable
- High contrast for gold borders to stand out

**Display:** Premium 3D tilt effect with mouse interaction, gold borders, and floating particles
